Overview

FC Smitt is a group of Men who play soccer in the highly competitive University of Maine intramural 6v6 mens league. The team is currently in its inaugural season. League experts predict little from FC Smitt this year due to their new status. Currently Fc Smitt is 0-1-1 And they sit in 3rd in division A.

The Great Cheating Scandal of 2018

Following a 2018 game vs the Desert Wolves which FC Smitt lost 5-1, it came to light that the Desert wolves cheated. Though no one knows exactly how they cheated it was clear they did. Currently the leading theory is widespread doping within the Wolves team. Though it has been acknowledged that the the Desert Wolves could just be better than FC Smitt this is seen as a highly unlikely option.
